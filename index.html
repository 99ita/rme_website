How it Works

  1. Information Gathering:

    2. To create a compelling email on your behalf, we kindly ask you to provide us with some key information about your achievements, aspirations, and any specific details you'd like to highlight. This will help us tailor the email to best represent your unique profile.

  2. Email Content Approval:

    3. Once we've crafted the content, we will share the draft with you for your review. Your feedback is crucial, and any changes you suggest will be incorporated to ensure the email aligns perfectly with your preferences.

  3. Generate Email Credentials:

    4. To maintain confidentiality and security, we'll generate a new email address and password specifically for your athlete outreach. Rest assured, we will never request any personal information beyond what is necessary for this purpose.

  4. Send Emails:

    5. Once the email content is approved and the credentials are set, we'll initiate the outreach process by sending personalized emails to coaches in your preferred divisions and states. Our goal is to maximize your exposure and capture the attention of potential recruiters.

  5. Payment Confirmation:

    6. Upon receiving your payment, we will securely provide you with the login credentials for the new email address. Your trust and security are our top priorities, and we will never ask for personal information beyond what is necessary for this service.

  6. Wait for Coach Responses:

    7. With the emails sent, you can sit back and relax as coaches review your profile. Once they respond, we encourage you to engage in meaningful conversations to further showcase your talent and discuss potential opportunities.
