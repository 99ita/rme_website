About Us.

Recruiting Made Easy is a service that helps student-athletes connect with NCAA coaches. We create a new email address and password for you, write up a professional and concise email using your information, and send it out to every coach in the NCAA. The email recipient list is also customizable, so you can choose which states and divisions you want to apply to. Once the emails are sent, it is up to you to follow up with any coaches that reply to you. We want you to have full control of which school you end up going to, so we leave the conversations with the coaches up to you.
